Welcome to the Gallery of Paraguay's "Futbol de Primera" Clubs for 2023! Feel free to view the club's logos for more information!








Cerro Porteño is one of the most popular clubs in Paraguay.

-Founded on October 1, 1912, in the neighborhood of Obrero in Asunción, the capital of Paraguay.

-Titles won: 34 in First Division

-President: Raúl Zapag (2023-Present)

-Coach: Victor Bernay

-Rival: Olimpia







Club Olimpia is another popular club in Paraguay.

-Founded on July 25, 1902, in Asunción, Paraguay.

-Titles won: 46 titles in the First Division; 3 time Champions of America from Copa Libertadores

-President: Miguel Cardona

-Coach: Francisco Javier "Chiqui" Arce Rolón

-Rival: Cerro Porteño







Club Guaraní is yet, another popular club in Paraguay.

-Founded on October 12, 1903.; The oldest Club.

-Titles won: 10 in First Division

-President: Juan Alberto Acosta

-Manager: Rodrigo López

-Coach: Pablo de Muner

-Rival: Libertad







Club Libertad is yet, another popular club in Paraguay.

-Founded in 1905.

-Titles won: 23 in First Division

-President: Rubén Di Tore

-Manager: Ariel Galeano

-Coach: Ariel Galeano

-Rival(s): Guaraní, Cerro and Olimpia
